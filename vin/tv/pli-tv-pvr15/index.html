☢️ Warning: Sujato's views on Jhāna contradict the Early Buddhist Texts (EBT). His interpretation relies on the Visuddhimagga — a Theravāda commentary written roughly 500 years after the EBT — rather than the source texts themselves. His translations are widely used but his doctrinal views should not be taken at face value. Always verify with reputable EBT sources.
